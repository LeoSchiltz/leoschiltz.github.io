In-Depth Description

The Storm Scout Sniper Rifle is a powerful sniper rifle with very unique features. For a sniper rifle, the Storm Scout Sniper Rifle has a large magazine size of 6 bullets. The weapon also has a useful storm tracking attachment equipped to the weapon. The device will display upcoming storm circles. The Storm Scout Sniper Rifle is found in floor loot, chests, vending machines and supply drops.
