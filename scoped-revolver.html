In-Depth Description

The Scoped Revolver is a scoped pistol that is most powerful in medium and long ranges. The Scoped Revolver is a great weapon that deals less damage than sniper rifles, but makes up for it with its six bullet clip, better rate of fire, and better performance in medium ranges. Like most scoped weapons, the Scoped Revolver is no good in short ranges. The Scoped Revolver can be found in chest loot. floor loot, and the legendary rarity can also be found in supply drops.
