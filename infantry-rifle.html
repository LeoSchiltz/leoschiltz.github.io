In-Depth Description

The Infantry Rifle is a fast firing Assault Rifle that thrives in medium ranges. Unlike any other Assault Rifles, the Infantry Rifle is non-automatic. This is made up for with the rifle's fast fire rate. Another big difference between the Infantry Rifle and other rifles is the way that the bullets act. The bullet drop on the weapon can be compared to the Hunting Rifle or any other sniper rifle. This means you must position your crosshair ahead of where your target is headed instead of simply shooting at it. The Infantry Rifle can be found in chest and floor loot.
