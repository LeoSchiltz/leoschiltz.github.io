In-Depth Description

The Infantry Rifle is a fast firing Assault Rifle that thrives in medium ranges. Unlike any other Assault Rifles, the Infantry Rifle is non-automatic. This is made up for with the rifle's fast fire rate. As of update v8.2.0, the Infantry Rifle's bullets are no longer projectiles and are now hitscan bullets just like all other Assault Rifles in the game. The Infantry Rifle can be found in chest and floor loot.
