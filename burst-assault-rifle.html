In-Depth Description

The Burst Assault Rifle is a burst weapon that can deal major damage to unalerted enemies. The Burst Assault Rifle's deadly burst is most powerful on the first shot. The accuracy of the first shot allows you to have more of a chance of dealing damage to opponents. The weapon's statistics are similar to the Assault Rifle; however, it lacks the ability to dominate in close ranged combat. The Burst Assault Rifle can be found in chest loot, floor loot, and the legendary rarity can also be found in supply drops.
