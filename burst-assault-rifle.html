Item History

The common, uncommon and rare rarities of the Burst Assault Rifle were first introduced to Fortnite around the launch of Battle Royale in patch v1.6.0. The epic and legendary rarities of the Burst Assault Rifle were added to the game in patch v4.2.0. The common, uncommon and rare rarities of the item were removed from the game in patch v7.1.0 content update #3. The epic and legendary rarities of the item were vaulted in v7.3.0. The Burst Assault Rifle has only returned to Battle Royale in creative mode and in limited time gamemodes.
