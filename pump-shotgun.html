In-Depth Description

The Pump Shotgun is by far the most powerful close ranged weapon in Fortnite. The shotgun has the potential to eliminate opponents in one shot. Due to the damage that the Pump Shotgun can inflict on opponents, the fire rate and magazine size are not very good. I believe that a shotgun is a neccessity in your Fortnite inventory, and the Pump Shotgun would definitely be my pick. The Pump Shotgun can be found in chest loot, floor loot, and the legendary rarity can also be found in supply drops.
