In-Depth Description

The Hunting Rifle is a non-scoped sniper rifle that can be very advantageous in many situations. The item's fast reload times and smaller zoom than most sniper rifles in Fortnite can result in some insane plays! The damage that the Hunting Rifle deals to opponents can be compared to the Suppressed Sniper Rifle. The Hunting Rifle is found in floor loot, chests and vending machines.
