In-Depth Description

The Flint-Knock Pistol is a powerful handitem that deals massive damage to opponents. Although the Flint-Knock Pistol only has a magazine size of one bullet, the item is similar to the Hand Cannon with devastating structure and opponent damage. The strong kickback on the Flint-Knock Pistol can be very dangerous for the shooter as it may knock them off buildings or cliffs. Having multiple Flint-Knock Pistols in your inventory will allow you to fire multiple bullets quickly before having to reload. The Flint-Knock Pistol can only be found in floor loot.
