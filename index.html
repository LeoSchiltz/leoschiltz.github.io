FORTNITE GUN STATISTICS

Assault Rifles in Fortnite are medium ammo rifles that usually perform best in medium ranges. Magazine sizes range from fifteen bullets to thirty. All of these guns are automatic and have short reload times.

Assault Rifles

Submachine Guns in Fortnite are light ammo guns that usually perform best in short ranges. Magazine sizes range from thirty bullets to forty. All of these guns are automatic and have short reload times.

Submachine Guns

Shotguns in Fortnite are shell ammo shotguns that usually perform best in short ranges. Magazine sizes range from five shells to eight. All of these guns are non-automatic and have very long reload times.

Shotguns

Sniper Rifles in Fortnite are heavy ammo guns that usually perform best in long ranges. Magazine sizes are only one bullet. All of these guns are non-automatic and have long reload times.

Sniper Rifles

Pistols in Fortnite are light, medium, and heavy ammo guns that usually perform best in short to medium ranges. Magazine sizes range from six bullets to sixteen. All of these guns are non-automatic and have short reload times.

Pistols

Explosive Weapons in Fortnite are rocket ammo launchers that usually perform best in short to medium ranges. Magazine sizes range from one rocket to six. All of these guns are non-automatic and have medium reload times.

Explosives

Other Guns in Fortnite such as the minigun are found in this section. If guns do not fit into any of the above categories, they are placed into this one.
