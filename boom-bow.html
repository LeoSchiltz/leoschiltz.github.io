In-Depth Description

The Boom Bow is a dangerous weapon that can deal massive damage to players that are hit with its arrows. Similar to weapons like the Pump Shotgun, the Boom Bow uses shells. The explosion of the Boom Bow can still deal damage to opponents even if you haven't directly shot them. You can even accidently deal damage to yourself if you fire a bow too close to where you are standing. The Boom Bow can be found in chest loot and in supply drops.
