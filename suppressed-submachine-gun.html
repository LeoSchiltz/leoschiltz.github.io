In-Depth Description

The Suppressed Submachine Gun is a stealthy weapon that deals an exceptional amount of damage to opponents. The item's fast reloads and high fire rate make the weapon an excellent close range item. Due to the very low recoil on the item and the thirty bullet magazine, it may secure you a couple of medium ranged kills, but it is by no means a better medium ranged weapon than the Assault Rifle. The Suppressed Submachine Gun can be found in chest loot and floor loot.
