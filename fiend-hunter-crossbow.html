In-Depth Description

The Fiend Hunter Crossbow is a limited time weapon that is usually available to players during zombie events. The low damage the weapon deals to players is made up for by the extremely high damage the weapon deals to zombies. The weapon's seven arrow magazine can be reloaded for an infinite amount of times. The Fiend Hunter Crossbow can be found in chest loot and in floor loot.
