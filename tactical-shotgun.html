In-Depth Description

Although the Tactical Shotgun's damage is slightly worse than the likes of the Combat Shotgun, there are still many benefits for choosing this weapon. The Tactical Shotgun has a fast rate of fire and can easily deal solid body shots to your opponents. The increased spread on the Tactical Shotgun may seem like a disadvantage to some players, but to players with bad aim, it may help them land more shots. The Tactical Shotgun is found in floor loot, chests and vending machines.
