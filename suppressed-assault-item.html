In-Depth Description

The Suppressed Assault item is the suppressed counterpart to the Assault item. The trade-off between the items is damage and suppression. Although the Suppressed Assault item inflicts less damage than the standard Assault item, it is still a very deadly item. There have been many times where I have taken the Suppressed Assault item over the standard item. The Suppressed Assault item can be found in chest loot, floor loot, and the legendary rarity can also be found in supply drops.
