In-Depth Description

The Assault item is an extremely useful item that I find myself using in almost every single match of Fortnite. The item's ability to dominate in long ranges, medium ranges, and even short ranges shows how versatile the item can be. The item's fire rate and its exceptional headshot damage and reload speeds makes the Assault item the perfect item to have in your Fortnite arsenal. The Assault item is found in chest loot, floor loot, and the legendary rarity can also be found in supply drops.
