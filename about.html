FORTNITE GUN STATISTICS

About

Fortnite Gun Statistics is a convenient Fortnite website that allows visitors to view the statistics of their favorite weapons in the game of Fortnite. Visitors can click on any weapon they like and will be shown all of the current rarities for that item and all of the statistics for each individual rarity. The tabular information includes statistics such as damage, fire rate and reload speed. Visitors can also learn the magazine size, ammo type and spawn information of an item.

Whenever a new item is added, or an existing item is changed, I will implement the new weapon or change into the website within a couple of days. If you discover any bugs or issues on the site, contact me at fortnitegunstatistics@gmail.com and let me know. It would be much appreciated!
